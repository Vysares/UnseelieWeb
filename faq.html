Frequently Asked Questions

Sizing & Fit

How do I find my size?

Each product listing includes a sizing guide specific to that piece. For cuffs, measure the narrowest part of your wrist or ankle and select the size that falls within that range. When between sizes, we generally recommend sizing up for comfort.

What if I'm between sizes?

When in doubt, size up. Our pieces are adjustable within their size range, so a little extra room is almost always preferable to too snug a fit. If you're uncertain, reach out with your measurements and we'll advise.

Can I exchange for a different size?

Yes — we offer a one-time size exchange on non-custom pieces, provided the item is unused and returned in its original packaging within 14 days of receipt. See our shipping & returns page for full details.

Materials & Hardware

What leather do you use?

All of our pieces are made from full-grain leather — the highest grade available, retaining the full thickness and natural surface of the hide. Linings are Italian suede. We do not use bonded leather, split leather, or synthetic alternatives.

What about the hardware?

Hardware is solid brass throughout. Finish varies by collection — nickel-plated for the Classic and Nightshade, polished raw brass for the Regent. We do not use chrome, zinc alloy, or plated base metals.

Are your products body-safe?

Yes. Full-grain leather and solid brass are both well-established body-safe materials with long histories of use in wearable goods. We use no adhesives, synthetic coatings, or finishing chemicals that come into contact with the skin.

Custom Orders

Do you do custom work?

Yes, and we love it. Whether you're after different leathers, lining colours, hardware finishes, non-standard sizing, or something entirely bespoke — get in touch to start the conversation.

How long do custom orders take?

It depends on the complexity of the commission. Simple modifications to existing designs (different colour, custom sizing) typically add 3–5 days. Fully bespoke pieces are discussed on a case-by-case basis. We'll give you a clear timeline before any work begins.

Are custom orders refundable?

Custom and bespoke commissions are non-refundable once production has begun. We require a deposit upfront and will confirm every detail with you before starting work.

Shipping & Orders

How long will my order take?

Many pieces are made to order. Please allow 2–5 business days of production time before dispatch. You'll receive a shipping notification with tracking once your order is on its way.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide via UPS or USPS with full tracking and insurance. Domestic orders ship free. International shipping is calculated at checkout. Customs duties are the responsibility of the recipient.

How is my order packaged?

All orders ship in plain, unmarked packaging. There is no external branding and nothing on the outside that identifies the contents. The return address reads only "Unseelie Workshop."
